newLaTeXdoc v1.0
developed by Dmitriy Shishmintsev

OPTIONS:
    ~$ newlatexdoc add templateFolderName             - adds templateFolderName to templates
    ~$ newlatexdoc create templateName folderName     - creates a template folder with folderName
    ~$ newlatexdoc remove templateName                - removes templateName from templates 


templates are stored in ~/newlatexdoc
